MetaBatch - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MetaBatch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

The Extension stores user preferences (such as default mode, aspect ratio, and download settings) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

The Extension operates on meta.ai. We do not control meta.ai's data practices. Please refer to Meta's Privacy Policy for information about their data collection.
